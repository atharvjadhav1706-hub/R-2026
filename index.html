About Us

The Central Examination Board (CEB) is a premier governmental body responsible for conducting fair, transparent, and highly secure examinations across the country. Our infrastructure ensures zero-compromise credential verification.

  • Secure Evaluation: Implementing advanced encryption and OMR analytics to guarantee the absolute integrity of candidate responses.
  • Modern Standards: Adhering to the latest biometric security guidelines established by the Ministry of Education for identity verification.
  • Research & Data: Utilizing large-scale analytical tools to drive pedagogical reforms and evaluate long-term academic trends.
Our vision is to empower the nation's youth by ensuring a meritocratic, unbiased, and technologically advanced examination framework.
